Mighty

Mighty is a 2D platformer where you play as Alphonse, a brave knight. You'll encounter monsters, puzzles and traps while progressing through the levels. Inspired from my favourite platformers, this game was my school project for USTO University and was my first journey into game developement.

Play Mighty !

Incinerate

Incinerate is an action-packed Top DownShooter game where you control a little dude ( or dudette ) who can wield multitude of weapons ( each has its pros and cons ). Your main goal is to devastate and overcome waves of enemies to finally meet their boss. Will you be able to achieve it?

Play Incinerate !
